FAQ

Here are some typical questions (and answers). Didn't find what you're looking? contact us.

  • About
  • So what is it you do exactly?

    Remott is a new brilliant way of living life to the fullest, while getting stuff done at the office. We organize “Remote Sprints”, so you and your team can focus on work while having a wonderful time in the beautiful Costa Rica.

  • And why would we want to do that?

    We had many different teams use Remott, for many different reasons. You might want a change of scenery so you can focus on an important task, or want to get the creative juices flowing, perhaps your team is remote and you want to get together for a week and work, or you feel like you never get around to actual team building since work hours are so packed already. Ultimately - most team leaders who use Remott just have a great team that they want to reward.

  • Oh, then you’re a travel agency?

    Nope. Remott is more of a concierge service. Organising a “Remote Sprint” can be a really taxing task, and can take months of preparations and research - often with unexpected hitches that could have been avoided. With Remott, a few clicks and you can be sure that all the details are sorted out and taken care of, and you can enjoy the perfect plan for your team. 

  • Hmmm, what do you mean by “perfect plan”?

    Remott was created by developers, for developers. We understand how you work and what your needs are. We also understand team building, and how to strengthen your dynamics as a group of individuals working together. Finally we’re also big on having fun and experiencing the world and what it has to offer. When you “Remott” your team, we work with you to plan the perfect mixture of all those things in one amazing unforgettable week. 

  • Program
  • What does a week with Remott look like?

    A week with Remott includes first and foremost work. We call it a “Remote Sprint” for a reason - there are goals and tasks that need to be completed. On top of that there are different activities and team building sessions that happen throughout the week. Also, in Remott we know how much personal free time is important, so don’t worry, there’s plenty of that too.

  • What kind of activities do you offer?

    We have so many different activities to choose from, these are just a few examples: fishing, surfing, local delicacies feast, expert video talks, spa, meditation, scuba diving, snorkeling, night out, group dynamics session, board games, hiking, yoga and the list goes on. Also - if you have something in mind we didn’t think of, we’ll do what we can to make it happen!

  • What activities to choose?

    We already have excellent programs that prepared for you, for different types of teams and budgets. Just choose your preferences and you can see how your week looks like. Want to make changes? No problem! It’s all customizable, but at least you have a good base to start with.

  • Who will be with us? Will we get personal assistance during our stay?

    Depends on the plan you select - you will get dedicated concierge to guid you through the whole program from setting your schedule and activities to dropping you back at the airport.

  • Pricing & Logistics
  • How long can we go?

    A typical Remott lasts a week of programs. We had groups that staid up to a month with us.

  • So bottom line - how much does it cost?

    We try to make our pricing as transparent and simple as possible, however costs depends on the exact details of your plan, the rooms you’ve chosen, and the activities you have. We have set predefined packages with prices ranging between $300 - $450 per night per person. If you need a custom plan, we’ll make the changes accordingly.

  • How many people can we bring?

    Teams that come for a “Remote Sprint” are usually between 5-20 people. If you have a different number in mind, give us a call and we can discuss any special needs you may have, we’re nothing but flexible.

  • Our team needs to fly in from different places, do you take care of that?

    Of course, we can take care of any flight needs you have, according to a travel policy you define. You can also pay directly to our partners who use brilliant software and travel hackers to get amazing flight prices, or have it included in what you pay us.
