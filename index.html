Your best ideas might come outside the office

Remote sprint is a new way to shape company culture. We believe that in order to launch a successful project, to boost your creativity and to strength the group you have to de-attach from the office.
Our 1-2 weeks programs in a remote location where everyone meet include dedicated group building sessions and fun activities that will empower the team.

What included?

Switch the city lights with full moon, the lunch break with tropical cuisines and the office noise with ocean waves.

  • Accommodation. Relax and enjoy our world-class luxurious facilities.
  • Workspace Fast wifi, coffee, snacks desks & chairs to help you get things done.
  • Team building Grow your team using our certified guides and curated group activities.
  • Activities Work hard play hard. Surfing, hiking, yoga, scuba and much more...
